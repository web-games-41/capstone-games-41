Timmy Eugene

Role:

  • A user that wants to donate his extra Chess Board Game

Technologies:

  • Razer Blade 15 , Windows 11
  • Iphone 14
  • Moderate Proficiency
  • Love Technologies

Behaviors:

  • Cares about the community and love to share his blessings

Attitudes:

  • Loving, selfless, giving

Scenario

  • Timmy got a new set of Chess board and would like to donate his old chess board to the public since he doesn't need 2 of them.

Frustration

  • See families at the grocery store that can't afford games that they are eyeing
  • See parents make more sacrifices just to buy toys for their kid's birthday
  • Kids without toys since everything is very expensive now

Goals:

  • Help families obtain toys
  • Lessen the sacrifices that parents have to make just to get toys for their kids
  • Give toys to kids that needs it more than himself

User Story:

  • As a user of this app, I want to Create a Listing for my extra set of chess board

Use Case and Interaction Flow

Use Case:

  • Timmy decided to list his old pair of Chess Board

Pre-Condition:

  • Timmy Logs in to his Toss Me a Game app

Post-Condition:

  • Timmy listed his old pair of Chess board

Interaction Flow

  • Timmy selects the "create"
  • Toss Me a Game pulls up a form to input details of the listing
  • Timmy uploaded photos of the Chess board
  • Toss Me a Game displays the image on the form
  • Timmy puts the information of the game
  • Toss Me a Game accepts the input
  • Timmy clicks "List" button
  • Toss Me a Game lists the item
