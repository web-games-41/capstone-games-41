Deanna Lean

Role:

  • This is the end user on the app looking to acquire donated games

Technologies:

  • Samsung A03s Android Smart Phone, Windows 10
  • Casual user when It comes to computers, but proficient on Smart Phone

Behaviors:

  • Smart and interested in educational games

Attitudes:

  • A mom, looking for good and clean entertainment

Scenario

  • Deanna is a single mom with children she would like to get board games for to keep them home and entertained

Frustration

  • New board games are too expensive for her budget
  • She knows that there are plenty of families out there with board games just sitting on a shelf collecting dust
  • Her children constantly ask her for board games when she goes to Wal-Mart.

Goals:

  • Once a month try out a new board game with her children
  • Find a reliable source for board games
  • Seek educational board games

User Story:

  • As a user of this app, I want to look for games to receive from a donor

Use Case and Interaction Flow

Use Case:

  • Deanna heard from a friend about an app called Toss Me a Game where she can connect with people that wish to donate their unused games.

Pre-Condition:

  • Deanna downloads the Toss Me a Game app

Post-Condition:

  • Deanna will have acquired a board game for her and her children

Interaction Flow

  • Deanna launches the Toss Me a Game app and will press "Create Account" since she is new to the app
  • Toss Me a Game app will prompt Deanna with Create Profile page where she will enter her First Name, Last Name, Email, Password, Upload a Profile Picture finalize this process by selecting "Create Account"
  • The app will now lead her to the Home Page where she can scroll down through the list of donated games
  • Once Deanna arrives at a games she likes she will tap on the image which will route her to detailed listing
  • In the detailed listing page Deanna can view such things as Name of Item, Condition, Description, Name of the Donor and will have the capability to message the person that currently has the board game she wants
  • Deana now has sent a message to the Donor
  • Donor responds and they have made arrangements to meet for her to receive her board game
