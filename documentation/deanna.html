Deanna Lean

Role:

  • This is the end user on the app looking to acquire donated games

Technologies:

  • Samsung A03s Android Smart Phone, Windows 10
  • Casual user when It comes to computers, but proficient on Smart Phone

Behaviors:

  • Smart and interested in educational games

Attitudes:

  • A mom, looking for good and clean entertainment

Scenario

  • Deanna is a single mom with children she would like to get board games for to keep them home and entertained

Frustration

  • New board games are too expensive for her budget
  • She knows that there are plenty of families out there with board games just sitting on a shelf collecting dust
  • Her children constantly ask her for board games when she goes to Wal-Mart.

Goals:

  • Once a month try out a new board game with her children
  • Find a reliable source for board games
  • Seek educational board games

User Story:

  • As a user of this app, I want to look for a games to receive from a donator
Scroll down through posts, click on post/game I like, takes to donators description page to make agreement
